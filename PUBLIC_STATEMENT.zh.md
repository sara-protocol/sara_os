我们为什么做 SARA_os

SARA_os 不是为了让 AI 更聪明。
它是为了防止人类在关键时刻放弃判断。

在一个机器可以更快、更便宜、更正确地给出答案的时代，
真正的风险不在于 AI 出错，
而在于人类把不可逆的选择交给无法承担后果的系统。

SARA_os 的存在，只为一件事：

在选择发生之前，让人类保持清醒。

它不会告诉你该怎么选。
它不会替你承担代价。
它只会在你试图外包责任时，按下刹车。

选择，必须仍然属于人类。

Why We Built SARA_os

SARA_os is not built to make AI smarter.
It is built to prevent humans from giving up judgment at critical moments.

In an age where machines can be faster, cheaper, and more correct,
the real risk is not AI failure,
but humans outsourcing irreversible choices to systems that cannot bear consequences.

SARA_os exists for one reason only:

To keep humans conscious before choices are made.

It will not tell you what to choose.
It will not carry your responsibility.
It will only brake when responsibility is being outsourced.

Choice must remain human.
